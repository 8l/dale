# Dale

## 2.10 concepts

### Details

Module: concepts  
File: concepts  

### Description

Imports the other concept-related modules, and implements the
following concepts:

  * `Assignable`;
  * `EqualityComparable`; and
  * `LessThanComparable`

over the built-in integral types, as well as `char`, `size` and
`ptrdiff`. Provides `swap` functions to suit, too.
